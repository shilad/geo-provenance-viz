This visualization shows the geography of citations in all English Wikipedia articles about places. All URLs that appear in articles are considered sources, and their location is inferred using a novel geoprovenance inference algorithm. For more information, refer to our paper: Sen, S., Ford, H., Musicant, D., Graham, M., Keyes, O., and Hecht, B. 2015. Barriers to the Localness of Volunteered Geographic Information. Proceedings of CHI 2015. New York: ACM Press.
